#### Spell Terminology

##### Spell Aspect
A part of a spell indicating the various values and qualities of the spell including:
- damage
- size
- speed
- range
- duration
- target limit
and more such as summon stats, quality of created items etc.

##### Enchanting
Enchanting refers to spellcasting that consumes Spell Slots permanently, in exchange the duration of the spell is only limited by the lifespan of the caster.
Destruction of enchanted items results in backlash to the caster dealing tier damage to the soul, this restores the spell slot used however.

##### Spell Resistance
Unless otherwise stated all spell effects cast upon an entity with a soul can be resisted with a Soul check by that entity, consult the [[New Work/Magic#Spell Limits\|#Spell Limits]] section for difficulty. [[Named\|Named]] entities have advantage on this roll.
Success on this check results in the entity not being affected by that spell effect, the caster knows their attempt failed.

Unconscious entities or entities that do not possess a consciousness in the first place automatically resist every spell effect cast upon them. 

Enchanted entities can not resist spell effects cast upon them by their origin.

An entity that resists a spell effect does not know it consciously unless it is a caster and succeeds on their check by 6 or more, in this case the entity knows a spell was cast upon it but it does not know it's origin or intended effect.
