- Turn based
- Talk and Analysis at the start of each round
- all weapons use d6s
- Overwhelming
- players don't take damage to a stat until an amount of damage equal to their stat is dealt in a single round, this reduces their stat by 1, this scales
- damage is taken via stats: Body, Soul, Mind
- weapon attacks take one action but can only be used once per turn unless otherwise stated

### Physical Damage

#### Physical Damage Limit 
|   Size   | Limit |
| :------: | :---: |
|   tiny   |   1   |
|  small   |   3   |
|  medium  |   6   |
|  large   |  10   |
|   huge   |  14   |
| gigantic |  20   |
| titanic  |  40   |
- A weapon you use is restricted to the Damage Limit of the size of creature it was made for,
small longswords deal max 3 dmg.
- While using Heavy weapons one size smaller substract 1 from it's level.    
- 2 size difference is -1 to weapon property lv
##### Size Difference
You cannot deal physical damage to creatures 3 sizes bigger than you, tiny beings cannot harm large beings, large beings cannot harm titanic beings etc..

While using a weapon with a property that directly affects a creature against an entity 2 sizes larger than you treat it as if the property was 1 level lower.

##### Hands
1H - One handed weapons
2H - Two handed weapons
V - Can be used either as 1H or 2H having different effects.
##### Weapon Properties

| Property      | Effect                                                         | Req    |
| :------------ | :------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ |
| **Bleeding**  | 1 damage at the start of next turn to harmed                   | Body 1 |
| **Cruel**     | if it passes BT it deals 1 psychic damage                      | Mind 3 |
| **Defensive** | gives 1 armor while held                                       | Body 2 |
| **Heavy**     | weapon damage and limit 1 size higher                          | Body 4 |

| **Piercing**  | ignores 1 Armor                                                | Body 2 |
