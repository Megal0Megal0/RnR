### Body 
- power, control, dexterity, speed, endurance
You can have a number of [[New Work/Mutations\|Mutations]] equal to your Body score.

### Mind
- logic, memory, perception, investigation
You can have a number of [[Skills\|Skills]] equal to your Mind score.

### Soul
- charisma, willpower, insight, acting
You can have a number of [[Trades\|Trades]] equal to your Soul score.
