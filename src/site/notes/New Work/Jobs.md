Each job has to have:
advantages on actions related to it
roleplay functions
drawbacks and benefits
mechanical ability that makes the character very different to play
roleplay ability that makes the character different to roleplay

#### Thief
Ability:
Double your Body score for actions that require control, dexterity and swiftness
halve it (round down minimum 1) for actions requiring raw power, strength and might

Advantages on any actions related:
- Climbing
- Sneaking
- Stealing

Drawback: as an outlaw

What drives you to theft? 
What is/are your favorite thing/s to steal? 
What great enemy did you make during your career and how?
