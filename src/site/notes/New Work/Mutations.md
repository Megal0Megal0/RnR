#### Anomalous Mind

#### Beastly Intelligence X
You can use your Body score for perception. 
Add a bonus equal to: +X to your Mind Threshold, the bonus can be no higher than your Body Threshold.
You are incapable of learning a language, having deeper thoughts, and are not sapient.
Your Mind score is set at 0.
